How It Works

From village to courtroom — here's how cases flow through the Justice LK network to registered attorneys.

1

Villager Registers

A Field Officer registers the villager's case, uploads documents, and selects "Private Lawyer" routing.

2

AI Prepares the File

The system generates an AI case summary and formal court petition. All documents are packaged.

3

Matched to Your Profile

Cases are matched by district and specialisation. You receive the full case file automatically.

4

You Take It From Here

Contact the client, review the file, and proceed to court. Your fee is agreed directly with the client.
